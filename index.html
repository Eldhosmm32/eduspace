Trainings

GENERAL IELTS TRAINING

The IELTS General Training test measures English language proficiency in everyday situations. The test has four sections: Listening, Reading, Writing, Speaking.

ACDEMIC IELTS TRAINING

IELTS Academic test assesses your English-language proficiency at an academic level to determine whether you are ready to study at an undergraduate or post graduate level.

UKVI IELTS TRAINING

This test is for test takers wishing to study at undergraduate or postgraduate levels, and for those seeking professional registration in the UK.
